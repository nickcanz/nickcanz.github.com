Elasticsearch settings

Hi! This is a proof-of-concept to explore the concept "code as data". Your codebase could tell you so much only if it was easier to get at.

Elasticsearch is a piece of software with a lot of different settings you can tweak, but there is no one place that lists them all. I wanted to compile a full list of Elasticsearch settings. The Elasticsearch docs are great, but all the configurable settings are spread out over many different pages. I wanted to list all of these out to have a quick lookup and to help discover settings you didn't know about.

So, I used bblfsh to analyze the entire Elasticsearch codebase and get all the settings. Take a look at the code! It's definitely not perfect, but it's a good enough proof-of-concept to explore the idea.
